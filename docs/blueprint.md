# **App Name**: Guardian Angel AI

## Core Features:

- Real-time Health Monitoring: Continuously collect and analyze health data (heart rate, movement, etc.) from wearable sensors.
- AI-powered Risk Detection: Employ an AI analytics tool to identify abnormal patterns and predict potential health risks, such as falls or inactivity.
- Automated Emergency Alerts: Automatically trigger alerts to caregivers, family, or healthcare providers upon detection of risks or emergencies.
- Medication and Activity Reminders: Provide daily reminders for medication, hydration, and routine activities through the application.
- AI Social Companion: Integrate an AI-based social companion for voice/text interactions to reduce loneliness and offer mental stimulation.
- Caregiver Monitoring Dashboard: Develop a web or mobile dashboard for caregivers and family to monitor the elderly's health status and receive alerts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#89B6E2) to convey calmness and trustworthiness, inspired by the caring nature of the app.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E5EDF5), a desaturated tone of the primary color, to create a soothing and gentle backdrop.
- Accent color: Gentle purple (#A289E2), analogous to the primary hue, but with adjusted saturation and brightness, to highlight crucial notifications and calls to action.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, for clear, accessible body text. Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' a proportional sans-serif, which will give the app a scientific feel.
- Use clear, universally understood icons for health metrics, alerts, and reminders.
- Design a clean, intuitive layout with high contrast for easy readability, suitable for elderly users.
- Implement subtle, non-distracting animations for transitions and feedback to enhance usability.
